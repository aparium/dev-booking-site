Welcome to the First Carbon Positive Hotel in the U.S.

As the first carbon-positive hotel, Populus serves as a new benchmark for environmentally forward hospitality, meaning we give back more to the planet than we take from it. Whether joining us for one night or embarking on a longer stay, guests can experience our restaurant, offering seasonal American fare, engage with our collaborations, or take in the dramatic views from our rooftop bar & restaurant while sipping hand-crafted cocktails. Join us at Populus for what is sure to be the next vibrant social hub in Denver.

Stay Positive in Downtown Denver

Located in downtown Denver at the intersection of Colfax Avenue and 14th Street, Populus puts you moments from Civic Center Park, 16th Street Mall, and the Museum and Performing Arts Districts. Downtown Denver caters to the those who live a dynamic lifestyle, seamlessly blending urban excitement with natural beauty. When you’re ready for outdoor adventures, the nearby parks, trails and majestic Rocky Mountains await, providing endless opportunities for exploration and recreation.

An Architectural Marvel

Inspired by nature, Populus’ distinctive design is influenced by the growth patterns of Colorado’s native Aspen tree – Populus Tremuloides – resulting in a stunning design, sustainable and harmoniously connected to its surroundings.
